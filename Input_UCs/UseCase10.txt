1. Customer logs in to the food delivery application.
2. Customer searches for nearby restaurants.
3. Customer selects a restaurant from a list.
4. Food Delivery Application displays the restaurant menu.
5. Customer adds items from the menu to the order.
6. Customer specifies order details.
7. Customer specifies delivery address.
8. Customer specifies payment method.
9. Customer reviews the order.
10. Customer proceeds to checkout.
11. Food Delivery Application sends the order to the selected restaurant.
12. Delivery driver confirms the order.
13. Delivery driver provides an estimated delivery time.
14. Food Delivery Application assigns a delivery driver.
15. Customer receives order confirmation with the estimated delivery time.
16. Delivery driver picks up the order from the restaurant.
17. Delivery driver delivers the order to the customer's address.
18. Customer receives the order.
19. Food Delivery Application processes the payment.
