1. Customer selects the "Make Reservation" option.
2. System presents a list of available reservation times.
3. Customer selects a time for the reservation.
4. System checks for table availability at the selected time.
5. Customer specifies the number of people in the party.
6. Customer confirms the reservation.
7. System sends a reservation request to the restaurant.
8. System receives the reservation request.
9. Staff checks availability.
10. System notifies the customer of the reservation confirmation.
